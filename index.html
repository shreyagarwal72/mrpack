What is a .mrpack File?

A .mrpack file is a Minecraft modpack format created by Modrinth. It contains a manifest and references to mods, configs, and resources.

Why Convert to .zip?

  • Compatible with popular launchers like CurseForge, MultiMC, Prism.
  • Easy sharing and installation.
  • Works on more devices without Modrinth-specific tools.
